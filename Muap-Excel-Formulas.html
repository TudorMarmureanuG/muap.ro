Muap Excel Formulas




The formulas you can use are found in the formulas.txt file.



Examples:


1. SUM
Formula: =SUM(A1:C3) (sums the values from A1 to C3)
Calculation: 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5 + 6 + 7 + 8 + 9 = 45

2. AVERAGE
Formula: =AVERAGE(A1:C3) (calculates the average of values from A1 to C3)
Calculation: 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5 + 6 + 7 + 8 + 9 = 45
9 values => 45 / 9 = 5

3. MIN
Formula: =MIN(A1:C3) (finds the minimum value from A1 to C3)
Calculation: 1 (minimum value in the matrix)

4. MAX
Formula: =MAX(A1:C3) (finds the maximum value from A1 to C3)
Calculation: 9 (maximum value in the matrix)

5. COUNT
Formula: =COUNT(A1:C3) (counts the number of non-empty cells from A1 to C3)
Calculation: 9 (all cells contain values)

6. PRODUCT
Formula: =PRODUCT(A1:C3) (calculates the product of values from A1 to C3)
Calculation: 1 × 2 × 3 × 4 × 5 × 6 × 7 × 8 × 9 = 362880

7. MEDIAN
Formula: =MEDIAN(A1:C3) (finds the median of values from A1 to C3)
Calculation: Ordered values: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9
Median: 5

8. CONCAT
Formula: =CONCAT(A1:B2) (concatenates the values from A1 to B2)
Calculation: Values in A1 and B1 are 1 and 2, and in A2 and B2 are 4 and 5.
Concatenation: 1 + 2 + 4 + 5 = 1245
